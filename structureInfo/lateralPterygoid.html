Lateral Pterygoid consists of a smaller superior and larger inferior head.

Contouring Specifics:
Fat plane can be difficult to differentiate between the temporalis and lateral pterygoid so when contouring useful to reference with temporalis.

Function ▷
Depressors the mandible (opens jaw)
Bilateral activation: Protrusion of the mandible
Unilateral activation: lateral movement of the mandible
Acts with the medial pterygoid to rotate the jaw and aid chewing

Origin ▷
Superior head: Infra-temporal surface of greater wing of sphenoid.
Inferior head: Lateral surface of lateral pterygoid plate

Insertion ▷
Front of the neck of the mandible and Temporomandibular (TMJ) Joint.

Innervation ▷
Anterior division of mandibular branch of trigeminal nerve.

Blood supply ▷
Maxillary artery.
