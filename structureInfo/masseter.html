Masseter is a thick superficial muscle and the major muscle of mastication.
Two heads Superficial and Deep. Posteriorly the deep head is covered by the parotid gland.

Contouring Specifics:
Can be difficult to distinguish from superior border of parotid gland due to proximity.

Function ▷
Elevation of the mandible (jaw closure)
Deep muscle fibres assist with jaw protrusion
Clenches the teeth

Origin ▷
Superficial head: Maxillary process of zygomatic bone/anterior two thirds of the inferior border of the zygomatic arch.
Deep head: Deep and medial surface of the zygomatic arch.

Insertion ▷
Angle and lateral aspect of the ramus of the mandible.
Coronoid process of the mandible.

Innervation ▷
Mandibular division of the trigeminal nerve.

Blood supply ▷
Facial artery.
