Medial Pterygoid is a quadrilateral muscle with 2 heads; Superficial and Deep.

Contouring Specifics:
Difficulties can arise when distinguishing muscle from the submandibular gland caudally.

Function ▷
Elevates the mandible to close the jaw.

Origin ▷
Superficial head-maxillary tuberosity
Deep head-lateral pterygoid plate of sphenoid bone.

Insertion ▷
Medial surface of the ramus to the angle of the mandible.

Innervation ▷
Mandibular branch of trigeminal nerve.

Blood supply ▷
Maxillary artery.
