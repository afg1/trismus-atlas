Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) articulates between two bones, the upper temporal bone and the mandible. The TMJ consists of a dense fibrous capsule with its surfaces covered with fibro cartilage.

Contouring Specifics:
Bone window
Anatomical boundaries include the joint capsule and ligaments.
These structures are not well resolved on the planning CT system therefore for purposes of contouring we would advise the contours to include the bone only.

Function ▷
Depression, elevation, protrusion and retraction of the mandible.
Lateral chewing by alternately protruding and retracting the mandible resulting in rotation.
